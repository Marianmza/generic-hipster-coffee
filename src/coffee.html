COFFEE

We work with specialty coffee from current crops whitch is carefully roasted. Our coffee is fruity , juice and never boring. We bring you the best from leading european specialty roasteries, currently mostly from Square Mile Coffee Roasters , a multi award winninig coffee roasting company based in East London.

You can currently buy these beans in 350g bags
